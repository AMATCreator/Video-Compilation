Noh theater

There is beauty in art and Japan has a way of telling folklore stories of mystical Gods and mighty warriors in some unique ways. One of them is Noh, a traditional form of performance art dating back to the 14th century. In this theater restaurant you will be served a multi-course menu of your choice accompanied by tales of the ancient past, performed in original way. The concept was created because it was believed that hearing the tales of Noh accompanied by food, would be looked upon favorably by the gods and therefore bring the viewer good prosper.

Temple prayer

Many visit temples and collect 'Goshuin' stamps, but only few participate in an actual prayer ceremony or stay at a templestay for a few days. Are you curious about the life of buddhist monks, how they live, what they eat, how they pray? Let's take a look at the bell ceremony held prior to the prayer.

Sagano Scenic train ride

What a beautiful countryside with the mountains and the rivers...if only you could see more of it. How about a scenic train ride? You can take the traditional old train in Kyoto that takes you along the valley of the Katsura river, embedded between beautiful mountains, from Saga Station all the way to Kameoka and back.
